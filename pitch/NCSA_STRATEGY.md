# NCSA Integration — Strategy & Action Plan

## 1. Why the pitch changes when the target is NCSA

LakePoint is a venue play: foot traffic, tournaments, captive families on
site for a weekend. The pitch there is "monetize the people already walking
through your gates." NCSA is the opposite shape of business:

- **It's a recruiting platform**, not a venue. The asset is a database of
  ~3M+ athlete profiles and the relationships with ~40K college coaches.
- **The revenue model is subscriptions**, sold to families through inside
  sales reps. ARPU and conversion rate are the levers; anything that lifts
  either is interesting.
- **IMG Academy owns it.** Mental performance is already a core IMG
  competency — sports psychologists are on staff and part of the on-campus
  experience. NCSA has the distribution; IMG has the IP. The two haven't
  been stitched together into a product families can actually buy online.

So the pitch is not "drive walk-up revenue." The pitch is **"you have the
sports-psych IP, the athletes, and the coach network — we have the product
that turns that IP into a SKU you can sell at scale tomorrow, white-labeled,
without building it."**

## 2. The core thesis (one sentence)

NCSA's recruiting profile tells a coach *what* an athlete can do; an
NTangible-powered mental profile tells a coach *who they're getting* — and
IMG already employs the people qualified to back that claim.

## 3. What NCSA gets that they can't easily build themselves

| Capability | Build internally | Buy NTangible |
|---|---|---|
| Sport-specific clutch / decision assessments | 12–24 months, hire psychometricians, validation studies | Live today, 9 sports, all 9 in v1 within ~30 days of go |
| Coach-facing alignment scoring (Fit, Clutch, NTerpret) | Needs product + research + design team | Already shipped; rubric is in-app |
| Family-facing report families will pay for | Hard — IMG hasn't productized its psych IP | Already the deliverable |
| Sales rep ammo (a tangible artifact in the pitch) | N/A | The profile *is* the artifact |
| Coach distribution loop | Already owns it | We plug into it |

The "build vs. buy" slide should be the spine of the deck. The build column
is 12–18 months and a hiring plan; the buy column is a 30-day rollout.

## 4. Where NTangible plugs into the NCSA funnel

NCSA's funnel, roughly:

1. Free profile created (family or athlete signs up)
2. Inside sales rep calls → upsell to paid tier
3. Paid family uses the platform; profile gets surfaced to college coaches
4. Coach engagement → commitment → testimonial → next cohort

NTangible inserts at three points:

- **Step 1 → 2 (upsell ammo):** the assessment is a tangible, $10 add-on
  the rep can close in the first call. Low-friction yes; not a $2K
  decision. This is the wedge.
- **Step 3 (coach value):** mental profile rides alongside the athletic
  profile in the coach view. Differentiates NCSA from 247, On3, Rivals,
  who have stats but no psych layer.
- **Step 4 (retention / proof):** IMG sports psych staff can use the
  profile data as the intake artifact for any paid coaching engagement —
  turns a $10 product into a top-of-funnel for IMG's existing high-margin
  services.

The third point is the one to lean on. We are not asking NCSA to displace
revenue — we are feeding their parent company's premium service line.

## 5. Commercial structure — proposed

Keep the existing unit economics from the LakePoint deck as the anchor
($10 / $2 / $1 / $7), but reframe the buckets for NCSA:

- **$10 retail per profile**, billed inside NCSA's existing checkout
- **$2 to NCSA** as platform margin (or roll into their subscription
  bundling — see below)
- **$1 to the affiliate / referring coach** (NCSA's coach network *is*
  the affiliate network — this is the multiplier they don't have today)
- **$7 to NTangible** — covers fulfillment, sport expansion, IMG
  sports-psych co-sign loop

The $50K → $10K ad-reinvestment loop ports cleanly: every $50K of gross
profile revenue triggers $10K of directed ad spend run *through IMG
Academy's* channels. For NCSA that's free top-of-funnel; for IMG it's
brand-aligned media that promotes both.

**Two packaging options to put in front of them:**

1. **À la carte** — $10 add-on at checkout. Cleanest, lowest-risk pilot.
2. **Bundled into NCSA's paid tier** — included free for paid families,
   priced into the subscription. Lifts perceived value of the paid tier
   without increasing CAC. We get paid on activation, not on retail.

Lead with option 1 for the pilot. Option 2 is the expansion conversation
once activation data is in.

## 6. The IMG Academy "thrown-in" lever

Including IMG Academy on-campus use **pro bono** as part of the NCSA deal
is a strong concession to put on the table for one reason: it costs us
almost nothing and it gives IMG's product, marketing, and psych teams a
live testbed they can reference internally. That internal champion is
how a pilot becomes a renewal.

How to position it (not "free academy access" — that sounds like a
discount). Frame it as:

> "The academy becomes the validation site. Every athlete on campus runs
> through the platform, IMG sports psychologists review the profiles,
> and we publish a quarterly validation report co-branded with IMG. NCSA
> gets to sell a product backed by ongoing peer-reviewed validation from
> its parent company's clinical staff — nobody else in recruiting has
> that."

That turns the giveaway into a marketing asset, and it makes the IMG
sports-psych team a stakeholder in the deal, not a competitor to it.

## 7. The 9-sports-in-30-days commitment

This is a credibility-defining line in the pitch. Be specific:

- 9 sports live today in the sample assessment library
- v1 build per new sport: ~2 dev days backend
- Full NCSA sport coverage v1 within 30 days of signed pilot
- "Live today" includes the rubric, the report, the coach view — not
  vapor

Put this on a single slide with a calendar overlay: Day 0 sign → Day 30
all sports live → Day 60 first cohort data → Day 90 first co-branded
validation report. Concrete dates make it real.

## 8. Risks / objections to pre-empt

- **"We can build this with our own psych staff."** Answer: timeline,
  validated rubric, coach-facing UI, and the fact that their psych staff
  is clinical, not product. We're not the assessment — we're the
  productization of it.
- **"$10 will cannibalize subscription perceived value."** Answer:
  option 2 (bundle it). Test option 1 first to get the activation data
  that makes option 2 a no-brainer.
- **"Compliance / NCAA rules around assessments."** Answer: the
  assessment is self-reported by the athlete and disclosed to the coach
  by the family — same legal posture as the rest of the NCSA profile.
  Pre-clear with NCSA legal in pilot phase.
- **"Why now / why you."** Answer: 9 sports already live, 30-day full
  coverage, IMG sports-psych staff is the natural validation partner —
  no one else can stand this up that fast.

## 9. Action plan — next 30 days

Sequenced, with owners TBD:

1. **Re-skin the landing/pitch deck for NCSA.** Strip LakePoint
   venue/foot-traffic framing. Lead with the build-vs-buy table (§3)
   and the funnel insertion diagram (§4).
2. **Build the "coach view inside NCSA" mock.** One screen, NCSA chrome,
   NTangible mental profile sitting next to the athletic profile. This
   is the single highest-leverage asset in the pitch — coaches reading
   it sells the whole product.
3. **Identify the right entry point at IMG/NCSA.** Likely IMG's chief
   product / digital officer rather than NCSA sales leadership — the
   decision crosses both P&Ls. Warm intro through the IMG psych staff
   if there's any path there; they're the natural internal champion.
4. **Prepare the pilot one-pager.** 90-day pilot, à la carte at $10,
   one sport (start with the sport where NCSA has the deepest coach
   relationships — likely baseball or soccer), success metric =
   activation rate on paid-tier families.
5. **Pre-stage the 30-day sport buildout.** Lock the dev schedule now
   so on a signed pilot we hit the calendar with no slippage.
6. **Draft the IMG Academy validation-site memo.** One page, frames the
   pro-bono academy access as a co-branded validation program (see §6),
   not a giveaway.

## 10. What success looks like at each stage

- **Pilot signed:** one sport, à la carte, 90-day window, success metric
  agreed in writing.
- **Pilot win condition:** ≥X% attach rate on paid NCSA families in
  pilot sport; positive qualitative feedback from ≥10 college coaches
  viewing profiles.
- **Expansion:** all 9 sports live, move to bundled-in-paid-tier
  pricing, formalize the IMG validation program.
- **Strategic:** NTangible mental profile becomes a standard field on
  every NCSA profile — the same way GPA or 40 time is. At that point
  we're infrastructure, not an add-on.
